About Me

Type obsessed.
Amateur chef.
Word junkie.

I graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design and specialize in branding and design for print and digital media. I’ve worked on projects for American Eagle, Aerie, DC Shoes, Fender Guitars, Harvard University, Intel, Verizon, and more.

My work has been awarded and featured by local to international publications and exhibits including the AIGA 100 Show (2007-2017), Type Director’s Club, Print Magazine, Communication Arts, Graphis and Rockport Publishers. I served two terms as President of AIGA Salt Lake (2013-2017).
